Mexican Chicken

Nice pot of mexican chicken.

To make this wonderful mexican chicken, you are going to need a couple of things.

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Taco Mix seasoning
  • Jar of salsa
  • Crockpot

With all these ingredients, place the chicken in the crockpot with tinfoil under it, then mix the taco mix in a cup with water, pour the taco mix and salsa into the crockpot and turn it to low for 7 hours.
